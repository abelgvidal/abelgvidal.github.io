AI Eats Its Own Tail

Posted: 2025-01-20   |   Tags: IA

Seeing the evolution of StackOverflow, which seems to be declining due to the rise of ChatGPT and similar tools, I wonder about the long-term viability of projects like this.

On the other hand, where will AIs get their information if these projects disappear? Their answers could degrade significantly without such resources.

Could we be stuck in a cycle where AI destroys these collaborative platforms, worsens after losing them, then these platforms revive because this is information that is needed (and now is missing), only to decline again as AI uses their information to present it in a more convenient way? And so on, in an endless loop?
